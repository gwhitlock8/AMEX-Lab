Platform Foundation  —  Shared Infrastructure
🧠

Now LLM Service

ServiceNow-hosted LLM infrastructure. Routes requests to the right model with built-in caching, rate management, and SLA guarantees.

🔌

Generative AI Controller

Connect external LLMs — Azure OpenAI, OpenAI GPT-4, Cohere, Google Vertex AI, or a custom endpoint. Manage credentials and routing.

🛡️

AI Control Tower

Platform-wide governance. Monitor usage, configure guardrails, manage content filters, and audit every AI interaction across all products.

🤝

AI Agent Orchestrator

Coordinates multi-agent workflows. Manages handoffs between agents, resolves conflicts, and tracks state across long-running agentic tasks.
